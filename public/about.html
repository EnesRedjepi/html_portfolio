About Us

VERA LUX Home is an online store from Skopje,North Macedonia which has its beginnings from the end of 2020, at the time when our country and the whole world faced the pandemic (Covid19) and at that time when buying and selling was most necessary online, at that time VERA LUX was created and immediately started with many requests.

VERA LUX Home offers home accessories, table sets, dining sets, pillowcases, towels, etc. Mainly our products originate from Turkey and we also guarantee the high quality of the products. Since year after year, our online store is only increasing its demand locally and widely as a result of the quality and affordable price.

VERA LUX Home as an online store made up of a wonderful staff and in customer service almost 24/7, promises you for other innovations in the future, everything related to the decor for your home.
